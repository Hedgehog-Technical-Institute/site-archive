Harmony Framework

Harmony Framework is a powerful and simple to use framework made in GameMaker: Studio 2. Its goal is to adapt the accuracy of the MEGA Drive titles while housing a slew of additional content and bugfixes from the original games, as well as create an essential, user friendly framework for beginner developers.
