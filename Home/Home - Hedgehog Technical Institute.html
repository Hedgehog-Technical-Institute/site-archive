About

The Hedgehog Technical Institute is an indie and amateur game development community founded on the principle of collaboration, and respect and to celebrate developers of all skills and specialities. The community is a great place to meet fellow game developers and share your passion for creating games. H.T.I hosts a range of events throughout the year including game jams, showcases, games nights, and developer talks.
